01 / About

Antonio Martin - TxnZee

Senior Creative Editor & Visual Storyteller

I’m a 19 year old editor who’s been editing for around 5 years now. I originally started just messing around with clips and effects for fun, but over time it turned into something I genuinely enjoy and take seriously.

Most of my work is focused on cinematic edits, smooth motion, pacing, and creating visuals that actually feel clean and memorable. I like experimenting with different styles and finding ways to make edits feel more immersive instead of overdone.

Over the years I’ve worked with different creators and online communities on edits, short-form content, and visual projects. Working with different styles and ideas helped me improve a lot creatively and taught me how to adapt my editing to match different visions while still keeping my own style.
